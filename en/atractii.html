Museums in Iași

Palace of Culture

Ticket Price: 30.00 RON

Hosts 4 museums: History, Art, Ethnography, and Science. It is the iconic symbol of the city.

View details

"Vasile Pogor" Museum - Junimea House

Ticket Price: 15.00 RON

Headquarters of the "Junimea" society, where Eminescu and Creangă used to read their works.

View details

"Ion Creangă" Museum – Bojdeuca

Ticket Price: 15.00 RON

The first memorial house in Romania, where the great storyteller lived and wrote.

View details

"Queen Mary" Municipal Museum

Ticket Price: 20.00 RON

Located in a beautiful historic house, it presents the city's history and its royal connection.

View details

Union Museum

Ticket Price: 15.00 RON

Former residence of Prince Al. I. Cuza, where the foundations of modern Romania were laid.

View details

Metropolitan Museum

Ticket Price: 30.00 RON

A unique underground museum under the Cathedral, focused on sacred art and history.

View details

"Mihai Eminescu" Museum

Ticket Price: 15.00 RON

Located in Copou Park near the Holy Linden Tree, dedicated to the national poet's life.

View details

"Stefan Procopiu" Science and Tech Museum

Ticket Price: 15.00 RON

Located in the Palace of Culture, it features early sound recording and old mechanisms.

Tourist Attractions in Iași

"Anastasie Fătu" Botanical Garden

Ticket Price: 15.00 RON

The oldest and largest in the country; a wonderful place for nature walks.

View details

Copou Park

Ticket Price: FREE

The oldest park in Iași, home to Eminescu's Linden Tree and the Obelisk of Lions.

View details

Palas Complex (Palas Garden)

Ticket Price: FREE

A modern relaxation area with fountains and a panoramic view of the Palace of Culture.

View details

Metropolitan Cathedral

Ticket Price: FREE

A symbol of Romanian spirituality, home to the relics of Saint Parascheva.

View details

Golia Monastery (Golia Tower)

Ticket Price: 5.00 RON (Tower)

A 17th-century fortified monastery. You can climb the tower for a city-wide view.

View details

"Three Hierarchs" Monastery

Ticket Price: 10.00 RON

Famous for its unique stone lacework on the exterior walls, an architectural masterpiece.

View details

"Vasile Alecsandri" National Theater

Ticket Price: FREE (Exterior)

One of the most beautiful theater buildings in the world, according to international rankings.
